Quality Certifications

Our duvets undergo rigorous testing to ensure the highest standards of quality, safety, and performance.

Testing & Certifications

OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 Certified

All our duvets are certified free from harmful substances and chemicals, ensuring they are safe for you and your family.

Certified by independent international testing institutes for textile ecology.

Hypoallergenic Testing

Clinically tested and proven to be hypoallergenic, making our duvets ideal for allergy sufferers and sensitive skin.

Tested against common allergens including dust mites, mold, and bacteria.

Durability & Longevity Testing

Our duvets undergo extensive wash cycle testing to ensure they maintain their quality, shape, and comfort through years of use.

Certified to withstand 50+ wash cycles without significant degradation.

Flame Retardant Standards

Compliant with international fire safety standards, providing peace of mind for your household safety.

Meets BS 7177 and EN 14878 safety requirements for bedding products.

Our duvets feature intelligent thermal technology that adapts to your body temperature, keeping you comfortable throughout the night.

Seasonal Adaptability

Engineered for year-round comfort with superior insulation properties that work in both warm and cool conditions.
