Hospital Textiles by WIMS

At WIMS Textiles, we understand that hospital environments demand fabrics that balance comfort, durability, and strict hygiene standards. Our specialized healthcare textile line combines premium materials with advanced manufacturing processes to ensure both patient comfort and institutional reliability.

1. Hospital Bed Sheets

Crafted from high-thread-count cotton blends, our hospital bed sheets are soft to the touch yet resilient to industrial washing cycles. They maintain color stability and integrity through repeated sterilization, offering both functionality and comfort to patients and healthcare professionals.

  • High breathability and moisture control
  • Durable weave suitable for high-frequency laundering
  • Available in clinical whites and institutional shades

2. Hospital Towels

Our hospital towels are engineered for maximum absorbency and rapid drying. WIMS ensures every towel meets the strictest hygienic requirements without compromising on softness. Perfect for patients, medical staff, and facility use alike.

  • High GSM terry fabric for superior absorbency
  • Bleach-safe and fast-drying composition
  • Designed for sterilization and long service life

3. Scrubs (Men & Women)

WIMS medical scrubs combine comfort, mobility, and professionalism. Our gender-tailored fits are made from breathable, wrinkle-resistant fabrics that allow healthcare professionals to move freely while maintaining a polished look throughout their shift.

  • Ergonomic cuts for unrestricted movement
  • Fade-resistant colors for a clean professional appearance
  • Antimicrobial fabric finish for enhanced hygiene

4. Doctor Coats

Precision-tailored with a refined finish, WIMS doctor coats symbolize both professionalism and reliability. Crafted with high-quality cotton-polyester blends, they provide breathability, easy maintenance, and a crisp, elegant appearance throughout long hours of duty.

  • Wrinkle-resistant and breathable material
  • Durable stitching for long-lasting wear
  • Available in traditional white and custom-fit options

Every WIMS hospital textile reflects our commitment to quality, comfort, and trust — ensuring that healthcare professionals and patients alike experience the excellence of WIMS craftsmanship.
