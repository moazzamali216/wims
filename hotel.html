Material & Fabric Innovations

At WIMS Textiles, we combine precision weaving, premium fibers, and eco-conscious dyeing to create fabrics that not only feel luxurious but perform beautifully under hospitality demands.

Luxury Cotton Blend

Engineered for softness and resilience, our cotton-rich fabrics maintain a fresh and refined feel even after repeated use.

Advanced Weaving Technology

Using modern jacquard and terry weaving techniques, we ensure precise density, exceptional absorbency, and a smooth, elegant drape.

Eco-Friendly Dye Process

Our dyeing methods minimize water use and chemical waste while delivering consistent, long-lasting hues that retain their brilliance.

Our Hotel Textile Range

At Wims Textiles, every fabric is crafted with a vision — to enhance the hospitality experience through timeless elegance, unmatched comfort, and lasting quality. Our hotel textile range reflects our commitment to excellence and innovation in every thread.

Bed Linens

Indulge your guests in a world of comfort with our finely woven bed linens. Crafted from premium cotton blends, our sheets and duvet covers provide an ultra-smooth touch and a crisp, fresh feel. Designed for durability and easy care, they retain their luxurious finish even after repeated washes — perfect for hotels that value both elegance and efficiency.

Bathrobes

Elevate relaxation with our plush hotel bathrobes — soft, absorbent, and irresistibly comfortable. Available in a variety of weaves and styles, each robe combines modern design with classic comfort. Whether for in-room lounging or spa areas, Wims bathrobes embody refined indulgence.

Towels

Experience true softness with our range of hotel towels, engineered for superior absorbency and long-term use. Their dense loops and fine fibers ensure quick drying and lasting plushness — an essential blend of function and luxury that your guests will notice instantly.

Curtain

Designed to complement modern hotel interiors, our Curtain strike the perfect balance between aesthetics and utility. Available in light-filtering and blackout varieties, they enhance privacy, ambience, and energy efficiency while maintaining a refined visual appeal.

Mattress Protectors

Built for hygiene and comfort, our mattress protectors feature breathable, waterproof layers that safeguard mattresses from spills and wear. Lightweight yet durable, they ensure a consistently fresh sleeping environment without compromising on comfort.
