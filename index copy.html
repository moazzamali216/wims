01. About us

The Best Solution for all Industrial & Factory Businesses

In 1999, Wims Textiles entered the textile sector with state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities for cotton yarn. By combining modern technology with skilled manpower in a uniquely inspiring environment, this new venture quickly rose to the forefront of the local textile industry. Today, Wims Textiles operates one of the most sophisticated and vertically integrated production setups in the region.
