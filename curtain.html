Wims Curtain Manufacturing Process

From concept to completion, WIMS ensures every curtain reflects precision, luxury, and lasting quality.

1. Fabric Selection

We handpick premium fabrics — velvet, linen, sheer, and blackout — ensuring optimal texture, durability, and aesthetic appeal for every design.

2. Precision Cutting

Our skilled craftsmen cut each fabric with exact measurements to achieve perfect drape, flow, and alignment for all curtain types.

3. Tailoring & Stitching

Using advanced techniques and meticulous attention to detail, we sew each curtain to maintain strength, neat finishes, and elegant pleats.

4. Quality Inspection

Every curtain undergoes rigorous inspection for stitching, colorfastness, and fabric integrity to ensure it meets our premium standards.

5. Finishing & Packaging

We finish with hems, headers, and optional embellishments, then package carefully to preserve quality during delivery.

6. Distribution & Delivery

Finally, the curtains are shipped to our stores or directly to clients, ensuring a premium unboxing experience that matches WIMS standards.

Curtain Manufacturing Insights:
Sizing, Dyeing & Weaving

Learn the expert techniques we use in crafting curtains that are visually stunning, functional, and tailored to your space.

Curtains & Draperies Sizing Advice

  • Measure your windows accurately — width and height — for a perfect fit.
  • Consider curtain fullness: 1.5x to 2x the window width for a luxurious drape.
  • Choose drop length according to style: floor-length, sill-length, or tailored.
  • Adjust for hardware placement and curtain rod type.

Dyeing & Printing Techniques

  • We use high-quality dyeing methods for vibrant, long-lasting colors.
  • Printing techniques include digital, screen, and block printing for custom patterns.
  • Eco-friendly dyes ensure safe and sustainable production.
  • Careful color matching ensures consistency across all curtain pieces.

Weaving Techniques for Curtains

  • Jacquard, plain, satin, and dobby weaving techniques for varied textures and patterns.
  • Precision weaving ensures uniformity and durability of fabric.
  • Textured weaves add depth and richness to your curtains.
  • Combination of weaving styles allows custom designs and finishes.

At WIMS, every curtain is crafted with precision and expertise — from measurements to weaving to the final color finish — ensuring quality and style in every piece.
