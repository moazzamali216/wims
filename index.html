01. About us

High-quality textiles for lasting impressions.

Since 1999, Wims Textiles has been weaving innovation into every thread. With state-of-the-art cotton yarn facilities, advanced technology, and a talented workforce, we rapidly grew into a trusted name in the textile industry. Now, Wims Textiles operates one of the region’s most advanced and vertically integrated production setups—delivering quality, consistency, and excellence at every stage.

  • State-of-the-art production facilities with skilled professionals

  • High-quality bed sets, towels, and apparel for wholesale

  • Eco-conscious and sustainable manufacturing

  • Custom design and large-volume production expertise
