Founded in Lahore, Pakistan in 1992, we have grown from a supplier of high-quality textile materials into a full-spectrum provider of integrated textile and apparel supply chain solutions. As industry pioneers, we achieved ISO certification in 1997 and have maintained it ever since, reflecting our unwavering commitment to operational excellence, quality assurance, and long-term client success.

QUALITY ASSURANCE
  • Implementation of rigorous quality control protocols to ensure consistency across all products.
