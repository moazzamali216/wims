About Wims Textiles

Wims Textiles | Bed linen since 1970

For more than five decades, Wims Textiles BV has stood as a trusted name in the textile industry. What started as a small, family-run venture driven by passion has grown into an internationally recognized company specializing in bed and bath textiles. Despite our growth, we continue to uphold our original values — quality, comfort, and customer satisfaction remain at the heart of everything we do.

At Wims Textiles, we focus on the import and export of premium bed linen, bath linen, and home accessories. Since our establishment in the 1970s, we’ve developed a strong presence in the bedding sector. Our goal is to offer a constantly evolving collection of stylish, contemporary designs under multiple brands, meeting various tastes, quality levels, and price points.

Innovation and quality are the pillars of our business. We consistently improve and expand our range to meet the diverse needs of our customers. With our broad selection, we’re able to offer the perfect balance between design, functionality, and affordability.

Stay connected with us through our website to discover the latest trends, new collections, and textile innovations from Wims Textiles.

Mission

Wims Textiles is dedicated to providing affordable and high-quality textile products that combine craftsmanship with contemporary design. Our mission is to merge decades of experience with a forward-thinking approach — emphasizing innovation, customer satisfaction, and sustainability in every fabric we create.

Sustainability

By 2025, sustainability will remain at the heart of everything we do. We’re committed to making our materials and production methods more eco-friendly while continuously improving our supply chain to minimize environmental impact. At Wims Textiles, we believe that even small, mindful changes — when done consistently — can lead to a greener, more responsible future.

Our Values

At Wims Textiles, our values are the foundation of everything we create. Each product is designed with care, crafted with integrity, and inspired by the belief that true comfort begins with respect — for people, materials, and the environment.

We are guided by three core principles: Quality, ensuring every thread meets our highest standards; Sustainability, reducing our impact on the planet through responsible sourcing and production; and Commitment, building lasting relationships with our customers based on trust and transparency.

These values have defined us for over five decades — and continue to inspire us as we look to the future, weaving tradition and innovation into every piece we produce.

An environmentally responsible office

Sustainability plays an increasingly vital role — not only in the products we offer but also in how we, at Wims Textiles, approach environmental responsibility within our organization. We place great emphasis on sustainable business practices and believe that both small and large initiatives together contribute to a healthier planet.

Some of our concrete actions include the strict separation of packaging materials, the use of electric-powered equipment in our warehouse, and the complete elimination of single-use plastics such as disposable cups, stirrers, and plastic fillers. These efforts help us minimize waste and reduce our overall environmental footprint.

Beyond environmental sustainability, we are equally committed to social responsibility. We operate under a collective labor agreement (CLA) that ensures transparent and fair working conditions. Additionally, we promote a healthy work-life balance by minimizing overtime and introducing adjusted summer schedules to ease the workload on hot days.

Whenever possible, we prioritize hiring local employees — encouraging cycling to work and fostering a stronger connection between our company and the surrounding community. At Wims Textiles, sustainability isn’t just a goal; it’s a daily commitment to our people and our planet.
